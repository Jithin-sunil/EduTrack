Empowering Student Progression

EduTrack-X is a unified control center for Four Year Undergraduate Programmes (FYUGP). It automates co-curricular activity registrations, monitors company internships, coordinates academic guidance, tracks research projects, and manages credits in real time.

Automated Credit Ledger

EduTrack-X features an advanced credit engine that automatically monitors and tallies credits from external activities, certificates, internships, and projects based on parameters defined by system administrators.

Dynamic Activity Hub

Faculty members can coordinate workshops, co-curricular classes, and co-curricular programs. Students register directly through their console and submit completion proofs to earn credits.

Research Project Management

Tracks research thesis progression from proposal submission and supervisor approval, through milestone reports, to final thesis review and grade submittal.

Internship Monitoring

Allows companies and faculty guides to coordinate internship evaluation. Students fill daily diaries describing work completed, which company mentors verify and faculty guides evaluate for progression credits.
